The Accounting Standard
LedgerAI Edition · GAAP Double-Entry
Audit-ready output from any file
Double-Entry Bookkeeping
Every transaction debits one account and credits another.

LedgerAI applies real GAAP rules. Every uploaded transaction generates a journal entry using a numbered Chart of Accounts — 1xxx Assets through 6xxx Expenses.

The trial balance verifies total debits equal total credits. If they don't balance, the system reports the exact dollar difference.

Audit Working Papers New
Lead schedules, PBC lists, and prior-period variance — generated automatically.

Upload a client's GL or bank export. LedgerAI generates formatted lead schedules and PBC checklists — the exact documents audit firms build manually at the start of an engagement.

Significant variances (configurable %) are flagged automatically. Export to PDF for the working paper binder.

Feature 4 — AI Memo Drafting

Client updates.
Written for you.

Claude AI reads your financial data and drafts the memos accountants write every month. You review, edit, send. Four memo types included:

Month-End Client Summary
"January operating expenses totalled $62,481, an increase of $4,320 (7.4%) vs December. The primary driver was a significant increase in Advertising spend ($8,200 vs $4,400 prior month), which warrants review. Payroll remained consistent at $37,500. Trial balance is verified balanced."
→ Client email ready
CFO Variance Memo
"Key variances: (1) Advertising +86% vs prior — management explanation required for working papers. (2) M&E at $696 is 50% deductible; receipts required. (3) Contractor payments to Jane Smith ($1,800) and Sara Okonkwo ($2,200) — confirm 1099 tracking current."
→ Internal memo ready
Management Letter Bullets
"• Advertising expense increased 86% without documented approval — recommend formal spend authorization policy. • M&E receipt policy should require digital filing within 5 business days. • Contractor agreements on file for all 1099 payees."
→ Management letter ready
Audit Planning Note
"Significant risk areas: (1) Advertising variance exceeds 15% threshold — set as significant for testing. (2) Payroll $37,500 — confirm register reconciliation. (3) No revenue recorded — confirm completeness of revenue recognition or note timing."
→ Audit file ready
